Frequently Asked Questions

Please reach us at pap.lorinc@gmail.com if you cannot find an answer to your question.

  • Q: Is POTUS Toss a free app?

    A: POTUS Toss is available for free,
    but certain features and coins can only be accessed by upgrading to the premium version.

  • Q: Why is it called POTUS Toss?

    A: POTUS is an acronym for President Of The United States,
    the app is focused on flipping coins with the U.S. presidents on them
    and Toss refers to the act of flipping a coin.

  • Q: What are the features of POTUS Toss?

    A: POTUS Toss is an app that allows users to flip $1 coins featuring every president
    and track their flips with a user-friendly interface.
    The premium version also includes access to coins featuring living presidents such as Carter, Clinton, Bush, Obama, Trump, and Biden.
    The app also allows users to see the history of previous tosses
    and the ability to randomly show a different president for every toss.
    It can also be used for educational purposes.

  • Q: What features are available in the premium and how much does it cost?

    A: A yearly premium subscription costs around $1 (for obvious reasons)
    and adds unrestricted access to coins featuring recent presidents.
    In the premium version, the coin never lands on its side
    and every coin is slightly bigger and more detailed.

  • Q: Can I use POTUS Toss for educational purposes?

    A: Yes, POTUS Toss can be used for educational purposes.
    Users can learn about the presidents and their terms in office
    while also practicing probability and statistics by tracking their coin flips.

  • Q: Is there a limit to the number of coin flips I can make?

    A: No, there is no limit to the number of coin flips you can make
    but the history only contains the last few ones.

  • Q: Does the app have all the U.S. presidents?

    A: Yes, the app includes all the U.S. presidents featured on $1 coins.

  • Q: Can I customize the coin's appearance?

    A: No, you cannot customize the coin's appearance,
    but you can choose from the different coins available in the app.

  • Q: Can I share my coin flip results with friends?

    A: If it turns out that people want to do that,
    we'll definitely add that feature.
